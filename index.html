Frequently Asked Questions

Will I actually win?

This is lottery mining. Odds per attempt are very low, but you get 52,500+ chances per year. Solo miners on CKPool won $285K (Dec), $347K (Oct), $371K (Aug), and $266K (Nov) in 2025 alone. It's rare, but it happens.

Why not use an ASIC miner?

ASICs cost $2,000-$10,000 and use massive power. This uses the Mac you already own. Think of it as a continuous lottery ticket, not a guaranteed income source.

Does it damage my Mac?

No. Apple Silicon is incredibly efficient. GPU runs at ~30-50W less than watching YouTube. Your Mac handles it easily without overheating.

How does 336x more chances work?

Powerball has 156 drawings/year. Bitcoin has ~52,500 blocks/year. Your miner enters every "drawing" automatically. More attempts = more chances, even if each attempt has lower odds.

Which Macs are supported?

Any Apple Silicon Mac: M1, M2, M3, M4 and all Pro/Max/Ultra variants. Requires macOS 14.0+ (Sonoma). Intel Macs are not supported.

Is the source code safe?

Source-available on GitHub. Thousands can audit it. No hidden miners, no malware, no tricks. Build it yourself if you prefer.
